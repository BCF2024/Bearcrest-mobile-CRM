BEARCREST CRM VERSION 8.2 - REBUILT

Changes completed:
- Professional dark green, gold, and white interface on desktop and mobile.
- Larger professional Call, Text, Email, and Directions controls.
- Improved desktop, phone, and foldable layouts.
- Lender dropdown now always displays a built-in lender list.
- Google Sheet lender list is merged with the built-in list.
- Backend lender reader now accepts common header names such as Lender, Lender Name, or Name.
- Empty or missing Lenders sheet is automatically populated.
- Duplicate lenders are removed.
- Existing duplicate-save protection remains enabled.
